Day 4 - To Mono Lake

vanomad

Category: California
Tags: Mono Lake

Little morning camp fire. Spent night on some public land off Bodie road --luckily I found!. Was planning on hotsprings near bridgport but was crowded.

Went for a morning hike across the road. I noticed the iceccles in a little creek I jumped across.

A view of my van from up higher on the foothill I was climbing.

Another view from atop the hill.

My first view of mono lake at a vista point as I decend from the alpine e on Route 395.
