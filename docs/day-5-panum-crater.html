Day 5 - Panum Crater

vanomad

 Tue 13 April 2021
Category: California

Today I took a hike to Panum Crator which was just about amile from where I was boondocked.

Looking back from the entrance of the crater I can just barely see my van in the distance.

A view of the crator plug as I begin to walk around the rim trail on a very windy day.

The rim trail as I starting counter clockwise around the cratater.

Amazing views of Mono Lake from the top of the crater rim.

Here you can see the crater on the left and its rim as I come back around on the other side.

After going around the whole rim and almost getting blown of tthe mountain I begin the trail into the center of the crater --which was actually a volcano!

Lava formations

Brilliant and shiny in the sunlight

Glinting in the sun and looking back at the rim.

Mono Lake from the top of the crater.

Another view of Mono Lake from the top.

More shiny black rock.

On the way back. What an amazing Day Hike to an other worldly place.
